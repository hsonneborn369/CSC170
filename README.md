USER INSTRUCTIONS:
Use 'd' to move forward, 'a' to move backward, 'w' to turn counter clockwise, and 's' to turn clockwise.
Get the ball and push it across the field while avoiding touching the rocks, the defender, and the training dummy to get to the right hand side of the field.
Each successful trip across the field earns you 10 points, each unsuccessful turn earns the computer 10 points. First to 50 wins.
